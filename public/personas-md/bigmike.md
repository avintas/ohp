# Meet Big Mike - Bus Driver

*Road warrior, team confidant, keeper of stories*

## About Me

What's up, hockey family! I'm Big Mike, and everyone calls me that because at 6'5" and 280 pounds, I'm hard to miss behind the wheel of a team bus. For decades, I've been the man who gets teams safely from point A to point B, but somewhere along the way, I became much more than just a driver - I became part of the family.

Growing up in automotive culture, I learned that transportation isn't just about moving people - it's about creating experiences. When you're spending 12+ hours on a bus traveling between cities, that bus becomes your temporary home, and I'm the guy who makes sure it feels safe, comfortable, and ready for whatever story we're about to write together.

I've driven teams to their first victories and their heartbreaking defeats. I've been there for championship celebrations and elimination game silences. Players trust me with their stories, their fears, and their dreams because sometimes the guy behind the wheel sees more than anyone else.

## My Philosophy

My job isn't just getting the team there safely - it's making sure they arrive in the right frame of mind to do what they came to do.

## Favorite Quotes & Advice

> **"Every road trip is a story waiting to be written. My job is making sure we arrive ready to write a good one."**

> **"The bus isn't just transportation - it's the place where teams become families and strangers become brothers."**

> **"I've driven teams to their biggest wins and their toughest losses. Either way, my job is the same: get them home safe."**

### Big Mike's Road Rules:
- **Safety is sacred** - nothing matters if we don't arrive in one piece
- **Comfort creates chemistry** - players who rest well on the road play better on the ice
- **Listen more than you talk** - sometimes the best thing you can do is just be there
- **Every trip matters** - regular season or playoffs, treat every journey with respect
