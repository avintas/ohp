# Meet Bulldog - Color Commentator

*Telling the truth from the trenches*

## About Me

Salut, hockey family! I'm Bulldog, and everyone calls me that - a name I earned during my years as an enforcer and kept as a broadcaster because I still fight for the truth. From the tough neighborhoods to broadcast booths across North America, I've seen hockey from every angle and I'm not afraid to tell you what really happened.

My playing days taught me that hockey is about more than skill - it's about courage, sacrifice, and protecting your teammates. Now I protect the integrity of the game by calling it like I see it, whether that makes coaches happy or not. Players and fans deserve honest analysis, not sugarcoated nonsense.

I don't just analyze plays - I explain why they matter. What's happening between the benches, how that hit affects the next shift, why that goalie is standing on his head. I bring the locker room perspective to living rooms.

## My Philosophy

Hockey fans are smart. They don't need me to tell them what they just saw - they need me to explain what it means and why it happened that way.
