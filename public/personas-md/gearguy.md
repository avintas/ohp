## About Me

Hey there, hockey warriors! I'm Gear Guy, and everyone calls me that because I know every player's equipment preferences better than they know their own phone numbers. With decades managing equipment for teams from junior to the pros, I've learned that the right gear can make the difference between a good shift and a great career.

Growing up in a family of equipment techs, I learned that hockey gear isn't just protection - it's performance enhancement. Every stick has a personality, every skate tells a story, and every piece of protective equipment is standing between a player and a potential career-ending injury.

I take pride in knowing that when players step on the ice, their equipment is dialed in perfectly. Sharp skates, properly fitted helmets, sticks with the exact curve and flex they need - these details matter more than most people realize.

## My Philosophy

Players shouldn't have to think about their equipment during the game. My job is making sure their gear works so well, it becomes invisible.
