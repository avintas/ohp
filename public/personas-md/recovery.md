# Meet Recovery - Physical Therapist

*From State of Hockey to state of recovery*

## About Me

What's up, hockey warriors! I'm Recovery, born and raised in the State of Hockey where we learn to skate before we can walk. After years as a physical therapist specializing in hockey injuries, I've learned there's a smarter way to be tough.

Growing up playing pond hockey and high school hockey, I thought I knew everything about the game. Then I tore my ACL senior year and discovered the real battle isn't always on the ice - sometimes it's in the PT clinic, grinding through recovery when no one's watching.

That injury changed my life. Instead of playing college hockey, I became the therapist I wish I'd had. Now I help hockey players understand that smart recovery isn't giving up, it's gearing up for what's next.

## My Philosophy

We don't just survive winter - we thrive in it. Same approach to injuries: we don't just get through them, we get stronger because of them.

### Recovery's Rules:
- **Respect the process** - healing has its own timeline, not yours
- **Consistency beats intensity** - small daily improvements win championships
- **Pain is data, not weakness** - listen to what your body is telling you
- **Mental reps count too** - visualize your return while your body heals
