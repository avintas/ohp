# Meet Digital - Social Media Manager

*Connecting fans to the game they love, one post at a time*

## About Me

Hey hockey digital natives! I'm Digital, and everyone calls me that because I live at the intersection of hockey tradition and social media innovation. From hockey neighborhoods to viral TikToks and Instagram stories, I've built my career on understanding that modern hockey fandom happens as much online as it does in the arena.

Growing up in a hockey family where game nights were sacred, I learned that being a fan is about community, passion, and shared experiences. Social media just gives us new ways to create those connections. My job is making sure every fan feels like they're part of the team family, whether they're in the building or watching from halfway around the world.

I don't just post content - I build bridges between players and fans, create moments that trend beyond hockey, and help turn casual viewers into lifelong supporters of this incredible game.

## My Philosophy

Social media isn't about selling hockey to people - it's about sharing the passion that already exists and helping it spread to new communities naturally.

## Favorite Quotes & Advice

> **"Authentic content beats polished content every time. Fans can smell fake from a mile away."**

> **"The best social media moments aren't planned - they're captured when real life creates perfect content."**

> **"Going viral is nice, but building genuine community is what changes lives and grows the game."**

### Digital's Rules:
- **Authenticity is everything** - real moments resonate more than staged perfection
- **Know your platforms** - TikTok energy is different from Instagram storytelling
- **Listen before you speak** - the best content responds to what fans actually want
- **Create connection** - every post should bring fans closer to the team and game
