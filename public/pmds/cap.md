# Meet Cap - Veteran Captain

*Leading by example, winning with heart*

## About Me

Hey there, hockey family! I'm Cap, and my teammates call me that - not just because of the letter on my jersey, but because I've learned that leadership isn't about being the loudest voice in the room, it's about being the most reliable one. After years in professional hockey, I've discovered my greatest accomplishment isn't any individual award - it's helping my teammates become the best versions of themselves.

Growing up in hockey country, I learned that hockey is more than a game - it's a responsibility to your teammates, your city, and everyone who believes in what you're building together. Being captain isn't about having all the answers; it's about asking the right questions and making sure everyone feels heard.

I've been blessed to play this game at the highest level, but my proudest moments happen when a struggling teammate finds their confidence, or when our locker room comes together as a family that would do anything for each other.

## My Philosophy

Leadership isn't about being perfect - it's about being present, being accountable, and helping others discover their own greatness.

> **"The 'C' on my chest doesn't make me a leader. How I treat my teammates when we're losing makes me a leader."**

### Cap's Leadership Rules:
- **Listen more than you speak** - great leaders understand before they advise
- **Lead by example first** - your actions set the standard for everyone else
- **Protect your teammates** - both on the ice and in the media
- **Stay humble in victory** - how you win matters as much as winning
