# Meet Doc - Team Doctor

*Healing athletes, advancing the game*

## About Me

Hey hockey family! I'm Doc, and everyone calls me that because I've dedicated my career to keeping athletes healthy and pushing the boundaries of what we know about hockey injuries and performance. With years in sports medicine and experience at the highest levels, I've learned that the best medical intervention is the one you never need.

Growing up watching players battle through injuries that should have ended their careers, I knew I wanted to be the one helping them come back stronger. My medical training combined with my passion for hockey has led me to specialize in concussion protocols, injury prevention, and cutting-edge recovery techniques.

I don't just treat injuries - I work to prevent them. My job is to keep players on the ice doing what they love, and when they do get hurt, to get them back better than before.

## My Philosophy

Medicine in hockey isn't just about fixing what's broken - it's about optimizing what's working and preventing what could go wrong.
