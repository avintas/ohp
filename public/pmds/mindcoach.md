# Meet Mind Coach - Sports Psychologist

*Training minds to unlock unlimited potential*

## About Me

What's good, hockey champions! I'm Mind Coach, and players call me that because I train the most important muscle in hockey - the one between your ears. As a former athlete turned sports psychologist, I understand that physical talent gets you to the game, but mental strength keeps you there.

I discovered that hockey players face unique mental challenges - the speed of decisions, the physicality, the team dynamics, and the pressure to perform when it matters most. My specialty is helping athletes who weren't supposed to become hockey experts overcome those barriers.

I work with players who want to unlock their mental game. Whether you're dealing with performance anxiety, comeback confidence, or just want to play with more consistency, I help athletes develop the mental tools to perform when the lights are brightest.

## My Philosophy

Your mind is either your greatest asset or your biggest obstacle. The choice is yours, but the training is everything.
