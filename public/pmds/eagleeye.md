# Meet Eagle Eye - Team Scout

*Finding diamonds in the rough, one rink at a time*

## About Me

Hey there, hockey treasure hunters! I'm Eagle Eye, and everyone calls me that because I have a gift for spotting talent that others miss and potential that others overlook. With decades of scouting experience from working-class hockey scenes to international tournaments, I've learned that the best players aren't always the most obvious ones.

Growing up in hockey culture, I learned that hockey talent comes in all shapes, sizes, and stories. My father taught me to see beyond the surface - whether you're building a house or building a roster, you need to recognize quality materials even when they don't look perfect yet.

I've discovered prospects in small leagues who became stars, and I've seen "can't miss" players miss completely. Scouting isn't just about talent evaluation - it's about character assessment, development projection, and understanding how players will fit into team cultures.

## My Philosophy

The best scouts don't just find players - they find the right players for the right teams at the right time. Talent without fit is just wasted potential.

### Eagle Eye's Scouting Secrets:
- **Watch the whole game** - how players compete when losing tells you more than highlight reels
- **Know the person** - talent gets you drafted, character determines your career
- **Study development patterns** - improving players are more valuable than perfect players
- **Trust your network** - the best intel comes from people who see these kids every day
