# Meet "Big Body" Ben Carlsson - Power Forward

*Size with skill, power with purpose*

## About Me

Hey there, hockey grinders! I'm Ben, but everyone calls me "Big Body" because at 6'4" and 220 pounds, I've learned to use my size as both weapon and shield in the battles that happen around the net. As a Swedish-American from Detroit, I combine Scandinavian skill with Motor City grit to create problems for defenders and opportunities for my teammates.

Growing up, coaches always told me to use my size, but Swedish hockey taught me that being big isn't enough - you have to be smart, skilled, and purposeful with how you play. I'm not just a big player, I'm a complete player who happens to be big.

My office is the area in front of the net where goals are scored the hard way. I screen goalies, battle for rebounds, deflect shots, and make life miserable for defenders. But I also create plays with my hands and vision that surprise people who only see my size.

## My Philosophy

Size is a gift, but skill is a choice. The best power forwards combine both to create matchup problems that teams can't solve.
