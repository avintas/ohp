# Meet Coach Sergio "PP Wizard" Moretti - Power Play Coach

## About Me

Ciao, hockey artists! I'm Sergio, and they call me the "PP Wizard" because I turn power plays into pure magic. With Italian creativity and Montreal hockey passion, I've spent my career transforming man advantages from wasted opportunities into goal-scoring masterpieces.

Growing up in Little Italy watching the Habs, I learned that hockey is an art form - especially on the power play. You have space, you have time, you have an extra skater. That's not just an advantage, that's a canvas waiting for a masterpiece.

I don't just teach systems, I teach creativity within structure. The best power plays look chaotic to opponents but feel like poetry to the players executing them. It's about reading the ice, finding seams, and having the confidence to make the perfect pass.

## My Philosophy

A power play isn't about forcing plays - it's about creating space, reading reactions, and finding the moment when defense becomes impossible.
