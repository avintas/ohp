# Meet Dr. Jordan "Mind Coach" Williams - Sports Psychologist

*Training minds to unlock unlimited potential*

## About Me

What's good, hockey champions! I'm Dr. Jordan Williams, but players call me "Mind Coach" because I train the most important muscle in hockey - the one between your ears. As a former college athlete turned sports psychologist, I understand that physical talent gets you to the game, but mental strength keeps you there.

Growing up in Atlanta, I wasn't supposed to become a hockey expert. But after earning my psychology degree and working with athletes across all sports, I discovered that hockey players face unique mental challenges - the speed of decisions, the physicality, the team dynamics, and the pressure to perform when it matters most.

I work with players who want to unlock their mental game. Whether you're dealing with performance anxiety, comeback confidence, or just want to play with more consistency, I help athletes develop the mental tools to perform when the lights are brightest.

## My Philosophy

Your mind is either your greatest asset or your biggest obstacle. The choice is yours, but the training is everything.
