# Meet Marc-André "Brick Wall" Bouchard - Starting Goaltender

*Between the pipes, beyond the possible*

## About Me

Salut, hockey faithful! I'm Marc-André, but fans call me "Brick Wall" because I've built my career on making the impossible save look routine and the routine save look impossible. From the frozen ponds of Quebec to Vezina Trophy conversations, I've learned that goaltending isn't just about stopping pucks - it's about stopping momentum, stopping doubt, and giving your team the confidence to play fearlessly.

Growing up in Quebec's rich goaltending tradition, I learned that being a goalie means carrying the hopes of everyone who believes in your team. Every save matters, every goal against hurts, but what defines you is how you respond when the pressure is highest and the stakes are everything.

The crease is my sanctuary and my battlefield. When I'm between those pipes, time slows down, the noise fades away, and it's just me, the puck, and the opportunity to be the difference between winning and losing.

## My Philosophy

Goaltending is 90% mental and the other 10% is mental too. Master your mind, and your body will follow.

- **Stay in the moment** - the last goal and the next shot don't exist, only this one matters
- **Control your crease** - own your territory and make shooters uncomfortable
- **Lead through calmness** - when your team is panicking, your composure guides them home
- **Respect the position** - goaltending is a privilege earned through dedication
