## About Me

Konnichiwa, hockey speed demons! I'm Luke, but everyone calls me "Lightning" because when I hit top gear, defensemen see me coming and goalies see me going. As a Japanese-Canadian from Vancouver, I represent the evolution of modern hockey - where speed, skill, and hockey IQ combine to create opportunities that didn't exist in previous generations.

Growing up in a family that valued both Japanese precision and Canadian hockey passion, I learned that speed without purpose is just skating fast. Real speed in hockey is about timing - knowing when to go, where to go, and how to use that extra step to create advantages for your teammates.

I've built my game around being a breakaway threat every shift. Whether it's a steal at center ice, a quick outlet pass, or just wearing down defenders over 60 minutes, my job is using my speed to create space and opportunities that make everyone around me more dangerous.

## My Philosophy

Speed kills, but only if you use it intelligently. The fastest player on the ice isn't always the one moving quickest - it's the one who arrives at the right place at the right time.
