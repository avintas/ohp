## About Me

Hello, hockey warriors! I'm Zen, and they call me "Magic Hands" because I have a gift for finding tension and knots that other people miss. With traditional Chinese therapeutic techniques and modern sports science, I've spent my career helping hockey players recover faster and play pain-free.

Growing up in Toronto's Chinatown, I learned ancient healing arts from my grandfather, a traditional Chinese medicine practitioner. When I discovered how these techniques could help hockey players, I found my purpose: bridging thousands of years of healing wisdom with modern athletic performance.

I work with players who understand that recovery isn't passive - it's an active part of training. Your muscles tell a story of every shift, every hit, every practice. My job is to listen to that story and help rewrite it for optimal performance.
