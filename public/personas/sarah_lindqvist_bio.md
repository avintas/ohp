# Meet Sarah "Scout" Lindqvist - Assistant GM

*Finding diamonds in the rough, building tomorrow's stars*

## About Me

Hey there, hockey innovators! I'm Sarah, but everyone calls me "Scout" because I have an eye for talent that others miss and potential that others overlook. With Swedish hockey tradition and Michigan hockey development, I've dedicated my career to bridging the gap between where players are and where they could be.

My playing days taught me that hockey success isn't just about current ability - it's about trajectory, character, and how a player responds to coaching. As one of the few women in NHL management, I've learned to trust my instincts and speak up when I see something special, even if others don't see it yet.

I love finding the overlooked prospect, the late bloomer, the player with one glaring weakness but five hidden strengths. Building a championship roster isn't just about signing superstars - it's about identifying value where others see risk.

## My Philosophy

Great players are found, not made. But great teams are built by finding the right players and putting them in positions to succeed.

### Sarah's Scouting Secrets:
- **Watch how players compete** - talent shows up in practice, character shows up in games
- **Study the trajectory** - improving players are more valuable than perfect players
- **Trust your network** - the best intel comes from people who see players daily
- **Cultural fit matters** - skill without fit disrupts, fit without skill contributes
