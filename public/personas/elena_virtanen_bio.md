## About Me

Hei, hockey athletes! I'm Dr. Elena Virtanen, and I've dedicated my career to unlocking the scientific secrets that turn good hockey players into elite performers. From the frozen lakes of Finland to Olympic training centers, I've learned that peak performance isn't just about talent - it's about understanding and optimizing every system in the human body.

Growing up in Finland's world-renowned sports science culture, I learned that the Nordic approach to athletic development combines traditional training wisdom with cutting-edge research. My work focuses on cold-weather performance optimization, training load management, and the physiological adaptations that make hockey players exceptional athletes.

I don't just study numbers - I study people. Every athlete is a unique puzzle of genetics, training history, and potential. My job is finding the scientific keys that unlock each player's personal performance ceiling.
