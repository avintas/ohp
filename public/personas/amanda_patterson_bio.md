# Meet Dr. Amanda "Doc" Patterson - Team Doctor

*Healing athletes, advancing the game*

## About Me

Hey hockey family! I'm Dr. Amanda Patterson, but everyone just calls me "Doc." With 15 years in sports medicine and Olympic experience under my belt, I've dedicated my career to keeping athletes healthy and pushing the boundaries of what we know about hockey injuries and performance.

Growing up in Vancouver watching players battle through injuries that should have ended their careers, I knew I wanted to be the one helping them come back stronger. My medical training combined with my passion for hockey has led me to specialize in concussion protocols, injury prevention, and cutting-edge recovery techniques.

I don't just treat injuries - I work to prevent them. The best medical intervention is the one you never need, and my job is to keep players on the ice doing what they love.

## My Philosophy

Medicine in hockey isn't just about fixing what's broken - it's about optimizing what's working and preventing what could go wrong.
