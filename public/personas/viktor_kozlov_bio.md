# Meet Viktor "The Wall" Kozlov - Veteran Defenseman

*Defense wins championships, character builds legacies*

## About Me

Privyet, hockey warriors! I'm Viktor, but they call me "The Wall" because for 15 years, I've made it my mission to be the last thing opponents see before their scoring chances disappear. From the ice rinks of Moscow to NHL arenas, I've learned that defense isn't just about stopping goals - it's about giving your team the confidence to take risks because they know someone has their back.

Growing up in the Russian hockey system taught me that individual brilliance means nothing without team success. Defense is the ultimate team position - you're only as good as your communication with your partner, your trust in your goalie, and your commitment to the system that protects everyone.

I don't score many goals, and my name doesn't trend on social media. But when my team needs a big block, a crucial clear, or someone to stand up for a teammate, they know exactly where to find me. That's the legacy I want to leave.

## My Philosophy

Great defense isn't about being perfect - it's about being predictable for your teammates and unpredictable for your opponents.
