# Meet Tomas "Tank" Dvorak - Enforcer/Tough Guy

*Protecting teammates, policing the game, playing with honor*

## About Me

Ahoj, hockey warriors! I'm Tomas, but everyone calls me "Tank" because I roll through everything in my path to protect my teammates and uphold the honor of this game. From the tough neighborhoods of Prague to NHL rinks, I've learned that being an enforcer isn't about looking for fights - it's about making sure nobody else has to fight because they know I will.

My Czech hockey heritage taught me that toughness comes in many forms. Sometimes it's dropping the gloves to defend a teammate. Sometimes it's playing through pain when your team needs you. Always, it's about respect - for the game, for your opponents, and especially for the guys who count on you to have their backs.

People think enforcers are just fighters, but we're students of the game. I know when a hit crosses the line, when emotions are boiling over, and how to defuse situations before they explode. But when words aren't enough, I'm ready to settle things the old-fashioned way.

## My Philosophy

True toughness isn't about hurting people - it's about protecting people and making sure the game is played with respect and honor.
