# Meet Maria "Miles" Santos - Travel Coordinator

*Making miles feel like home, one trip at a time*

## About Me

¡Hola, hockey family! I'm Maria, but everyone calls me "Miles" because I've coordinated more hockey team travel than a flight attendant has served peanuts. With a background in airline operations and a passion for logistics perfection, I've spent my career making sure teams arrive rested, prepared, and ready to perform.

Growing up in Miami in a family that moved frequently, I learned that travel can either drain your energy or fuel your adventure - it all depends on the planning. When I discovered hockey and saw how much these teams travel, I knew I'd found my calling: making the road feel like home.

I don't just book flights and hotels - I choreograph travel experiences that give teams every possible advantage. Time zone management, meal timing, practice scheduling, even bus routes to avoid traffic - every detail matters when you're chasing championships.

## My Philosophy

Great travel coordination is invisible. When everything goes perfectly, nobody notices my work - and that's exactly how I like it.
