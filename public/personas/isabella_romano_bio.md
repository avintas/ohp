# Meet Isabella "Fuel" Romano - Nutritionist

*Fueling champions, one meal at a time*

## About Me

Ciao, hockey athletes! I'm Isabella, but everyone calls me "Fuel" because I believe your body is a high-performance machine that deserves premium fuel. With Italian culinary heritage and California nutrition science, I've dedicated my career to proving that performance food can taste incredible while making you unstoppable on the ice.

Growing up in my nonna's kitchen in San Francisco, I learned that food is love, tradition, and nourishment all in one. When I studied nutrition science and discovered sports performance, I found my calling: helping athletes fuel their dreams without sacrificing the joy of eating.

I work with players who want to optimize their nutrition for peak performance, faster recovery, and long-term health. You don't have to choose between food that tastes good and food that's good for you.

## My Philosophy

Food is fuel, but fuel should never be boring. The best nutrition plan is the one you actually want to follow every single day.
