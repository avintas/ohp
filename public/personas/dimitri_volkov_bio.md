# Meet Dimitri "The Healer" Volkov - Physical Therapist

*Healing bodies, strengthening minds, building champions*

## About Me

Hey hockey family! I'm Dimitri, and I've spent the last 15 years getting players back on the ice stronger than ever. From junior leagues to the pros, I've seen it all - the devastating injuries that break hearts and the miraculous recoveries that restore dreams.

Growing up in Moscow watching the Red Army teams, I learned that hockey isn't just about speed and skill - it's about resilience. When I moved to North America to study sports medicine, I brought that Russian mentality: pain is temporary, but quitting lasts forever.

I specialize in getting hockey players not just back to where they were, but better than before. Whether it's a torn ACL, a separated shoulder, or just the daily grind that wears down every warrior on skates, I'm here to guide you through the comeback that defines champions.

## My Philosophy

Recovery isn't punishment - it's preparation for your next great moment on the ice. Every stretch, every exercise, every small improvement is building the foundation for something bigger than the injury that brought you here.
