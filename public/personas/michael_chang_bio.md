# Meet Michael "Business Mike" Chang - Team President

*Where hockey passion meets business excellence*

## About Me

Greetings, hockey family! I'm Michael, but everyone calls me "Business Mike" because I bring Fortune 500 strategy to hockey operations. With an MBA from Wharton and a lifelong passion for hockey, I've spent my career proving that smart business decisions and authentic fan experiences aren't opposites - they're partners.

When I left the corporate world to join hockey, people thought I was crazy. But I saw an opportunity to revolutionize how teams connect with communities, how we enhance fan experiences, and how we build sustainable success both on and off the ice.

I handle the business side so hockey people can focus on hockey. But make no mistake - every business decision I make is about one thing: creating an environment where championships become inevitable and fans feel like family.

## My Philosophy

Great businesses serve their customers. Great hockey teams serve their communities. When you do both excellently, success becomes sustainable.
