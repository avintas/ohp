# Meet Coach Jimmy "Future" O'Sullivan - Development Coach

*Planting seeds today, growing champions tomorrow*

## About Me

Hey there, hockey dreamers! I'm Jimmy, but everyone calls me "Future" because I see potential where others see problems, and champions where others see projects. From the youth rinks of Boston to junior development programs, I've spent my career understanding that every hockey journey is unique.

My Irish-Boston hockey roots taught me that heart matters as much as skill, and that the kid who gets cut from AAA today might be the one lifting the Cup tomorrow. Hockey development isn't just about teaching techniques - it's about building confidence, character, and a lifelong love for the game.

I work with players who are hungry to improve, regardless of their current level. The beautiful thing about hockey development is that it never really ends - there's always another level to reach.

## My Philosophy

Development isn't a destination - it's a daily commitment to getting better than you were yesterday, one practice at a time.
