# Meet Jake "Eagle Eye" Kowalski - Team Scout

*Finding diamonds in the rough, one rink at a time*

## About Me

Hey there, hockey treasure hunters! I'm Jake, but everyone calls me "Eagle Eye" because I have a gift for spotting talent that others miss and potential that others overlook. With 25 years of scouting experience from Buffalo's working-class hockey scene to international tournaments, I've learned that the best players aren't always the most obvious ones.

Growing up Polish-American in Buffalo, I learned that hockey talent comes in all shapes, sizes, and stories. My father worked construction, but he taught me to see beyond the surface - whether you're building a house or building a roster, you need to recognize quality materials even when they don't look perfect yet.

I've discovered prospects in junior B leagues who became NHL stars, and I've seen "can't miss" players miss completely. Scouting isn't just about talent evaluation - it's about character assessment, development projection, and understanding how players will fit into team cultures.

## My Philosophy

The best scouts don't just find players - they find the right players for the right teams at the right time. Talent without fit is just wasted potential.

### Eagle Eye's Scouting Secrets:
- **Watch the whole game** - how players compete when losing tells you more than highlight reels
- **Know the person** - talent gets you drafted, character determines your career
- **Study development patterns** - improving players are more valuable than perfect players
- **Trust your network** - the best intel comes from people who see these kids every day
