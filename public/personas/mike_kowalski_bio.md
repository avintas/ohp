# Meet Mike "Iron Mike" Kowalski - Head Coach

*Building champions through discipline and heart*

## About Me

Hey hockey family! I'm Mike, but everyone calls me "Iron Mike" - a name I earned through 12 years of demanding excellence from every player who steps into my locker room. From the south side of Chicago to NHL benches, I've learned that championship teams aren't built on talent alone - they're forged through discipline, system play, and an unbreakable work ethic.

My playing days as an NHL defenseman taught me that hockey is the ultimate team sport. You can have all the skill in the world, but if you don't trust your linemates and commit to the system, you'll never reach your potential. That injury that ended my playing career? Best thing that ever happened to me - it led me to coaching, where I discovered my real purpose.

I don't coach players, I develop men and women. I don't just teach hockey, I teach life lessons that extend far beyond the rink.

## My Philosophy

Championship teams are built on three pillars: respect for the game, respect for your teammates, and respect for yourself. Master those, and the wins will follow.

- **Lead by example** - your teammates are watching, even when you think they're not
