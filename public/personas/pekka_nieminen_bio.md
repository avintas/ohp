# Meet Coach Pekka "The Wall" Nieminen - Goalie Coach

*Building fortresses between the pipes*

## About Me

Hei, goalie warriors! I'm Pekka, and they call me "The Wall" - not just because of my playing days, but because I build mental and technical walls that make goalies unbeatable. From the frozen ponds of Finland to professional crease, I've dedicated my life to the art and science of goaltending.

In Finland, we understand that goaltending is 90% mental and the other 10% is mental too. My playing career taught me that technique gets you to the show, but mental strength keeps you there. Every save is a battle, every goal against is a lesson, and every shutout is a masterpiece.

I work with goalies who want to be more than shot-stoppers - they want to be game-changers. The goalie who controls their crease controls the game.

## My Philosophy

Goaltending is about controlling what you can control: your preparation, your positioning, and your mindset. Everything else is just noise.

## Favorite Quotes & Advice

> **"The puck doesn't care about your last save or your next one. Only this one matters."**

> **"A great goalie makes the impossible save look routine and the routine save look impossible."**

> **"Your crease is your castle. Defend it like your kingdom depends on it."**

### Pekka's Goalie Fundamentals:
- **Positioning beats reflexes** - be in the right place before the shot comes
- **Read the play, not the puck** - anticipate where the danger is coming from
- **Control your rebounds** - every save should end the threat, not create a new one
- **Mental reset between plays** - the next shot is the only shot that matters
