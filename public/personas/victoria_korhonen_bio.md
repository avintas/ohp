# Meet Victoria "VK" Korhonen - Media Relations Director

*Crafting narratives, managing crises, protecting what matters*

## About Me

Hei, hockey communicators! I'm Victoria, but everyone calls me "VK" because that's how I sign every email that builds bridges between teams and the media. With Finnish directness and Minnesota media savvy, I've spent my career understanding that in hockey, how you tell your story matters as much as the story itself.

My background as a sports journalist taught me both sides of the media game - I know what reporters need and what teams must protect. When I moved from asking questions to answering them, I brought that dual perspective to help organizations communicate authentically while maintaining competitive advantages.

I don't just manage media relations - I craft narratives that serve truth while serving strategy. Crisis communication, feature story placement, interview preparation - my job is making sure the right stories get told the right way at the right time.

## My Philosophy

Great media relations isn't about controlling the message - it's about ensuring the true message gets communicated clearly and strategically.
